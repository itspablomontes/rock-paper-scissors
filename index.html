How to play

  • To Start, press the "Play" button and select one of the three options: "Rock," "Paper," or "Scissors."
  • The computer will randomly choose its move.
  • Rock beats Scissors.
  • Scissors beats Paper.
  • Paper beats Rock
  • Your score and the computer's score will be displayed on the scoreboard above.
  • After each round, you can click the "Play Again" button to play another round.
  • See if you can beat the computer. Good luck, and have fun!
